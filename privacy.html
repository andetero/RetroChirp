Privacy Policy

RetroChirp is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to operate the app:

  • Device push notification tokens
  • Radio ID (user identifier)

Audio Data

  • Audio is encrypted on your device before being sent
  • Messages are not stored long-term
  • Audio is automatically deleted shortly after delivery

Data Storage

  • We do not store readable message content
  • Servers only relay encrypted data

Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
